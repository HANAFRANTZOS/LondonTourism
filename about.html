OUR PHILOSOPHY
LT is the bigest ane the older conpany of tourism in the UK.
Founded by Christodoulos Christodoulou, IQONA ARCHITECTS is a Cyprus Architectural Firm with fifteen years of experience in the field of architectural design and supervision of residential, commercial and industrial buildings.
Our fundamental principle is that great ideas are developed through sharing and collaboration. It is our conviction that the result of mixing good ingredients is often greater than the outcome that any one of these individual components can have on its own.
We are a team of energetic, curious, and experimental architects, designers and entrepreneurs, working together across a wide range of scales and typologies. Our passion for architecture and our shared knowledge allow us to investigate innovated ideas and create a remarkable experience for our clients.
Our approach to architecture usually comes across as simple, yet powerful and soulful. We seek inspiration in all facets of human life and nature, which in turn grants us access to a broad design tool palette.
To us, architecture is storytelling, using the language of the landscape and built form, shaped by the values and needs of its users. This fundamental aspect leads to a self-evident statement – that every project demands individual attention for a refined and unique result.
